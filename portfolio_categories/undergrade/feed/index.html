Arms residential blocks – Barcelona

This proposed mixed-use residential high rise structure is located at the heart of Barcelona (District 21). Large big balconies and a spacious rooftop terrace space are designed especially to encourage the residents to spend more time outside the apartments. The exposed central stairways is one iconic features of the design.

The floor plans of the apartments are carefully analyzed and designed keeping in mind the lifestyle of the people in Barcelona. The exposed thin water tubes on the exterior walls of the structure will help in regulating the temperature inside the apartments. The shape and structure of the pyramid play a significant role.

An Architectural Intervention – Dubai 

This cultural and heritage hub is proposed in the heart of Dubai, surrounded by buildings of historic and cultural significance. The hub was designed to allow maximum amount of natural light to the indoor spaces thus creating the spaces to be livelier and connected.

The shape of the hub is inspired from the seashell as the location of the site is closer to the port and also water played a significant role in the Arab culture as it was a means of living in the early years thus respecting their tradition and religion.
