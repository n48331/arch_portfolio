Evolution of Life – Time – Conceptual 

This design is inspired from the concept of time machine. I believe that time is the most precious gift one can gift to anyone. This special model here is a dedication to one my most favorite person in this world who is no more and I always thought I had enough time to spend with her. Now I can only wish travel back in time to revisit all the memories and relive them ones again.

Autocad drawings along with lasercut methods and hand skills are used to make this..

Unveiling the Complexity – Conceptual 

This design is inspired from the anatomy of a human body and its complexity – the ribs, backbone, brain. I was always fascinated with the abilities of human brain and the capabilities of human body to withstand the odds.

It’s an intertwining of architecture, technology and human science. This model was entirely made of all the left over materials available at the university workshop, The design process of this particular project was quite tricky since I kept adding elements and increasing the complexity depending on the availabilty and flexibility of the material.

Travel Diary Library – Conceptual 

The travelling library which move from one place to another by means of water. The design is highly inspired from ship mixing it with a contemporary yet minimalistic features. The different levels of the library is designed to provide both private and public spaces to the consumers.

The model is made of wood and photographing the model in different settings helped understand the play of light and shadow.
