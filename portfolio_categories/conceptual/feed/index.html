Evolution of Life – Time – Conceptual 

This design is inspired from the concept of time machine. I believe that time is the most precious gift one can gift to anyone. This special model here is a dedication to one my most favorite person in this world who is no more and I always thought I had enough time to spend with her. Now I can only wish travel back in time to revisit all the memories and relive them ones again.

Autocad drawings along with lasercut methods and hand skills are used to make this..

Unveiling the Complexity – Conceptual 

This design is inspired from the anatomy of a human body and its complexity – the ribs, backbone, brain. I was always fascinated with the abilities of human brain and the capabilities of human body to withstand the odds.

It’s an intertwining of architecture, technology and human science. This model was entirely made of all the left over materials available at the university workshop, The design process of this particular project was quite tricky since I kept adding elements and increasing the complexity depending on the availabilty and flexibility of the material.

Kemerie Observation Tower – Latvia 

The design has been sculpted by keeping in mind the prime visual points of the park, eventually resulting in a triangular shape. The main idea has always been to create a natural link connecting the existing pathways, so that it is convenient for the visitors to access.The slope of the observation tower is designed as a varied green landscape to view the incredible views of the magnificent landscape with both staircases and ramps making it easy for all visitors.

Furthermore the structure rises upwards as an extension of the ground plane which seems to be a part of the landscape but reaches for the sky.

3D printing exploration

The Other Side – Skyhive Design Challenge

Mountains are natural skyscraper, so why don’t we built in to the existing one without polluting the land thus making it zero foot print. But by doing so the natural inhabitants living in the mountain shouldn’t be disturbed rather we are trying to incorporate them to the design. Our design enables human beings to be more close to the nature.

We are trying to take a part of the already existing ecosystem into building without disturbing there cycle. Also by using water as the main mode of transportation the rate of air and noise pollution would decrease thus resulting in a healthier environment.
