Inter-modal Station – Beijing  

The infrastructure terminal is proposed to be designed at major inter-junctions of various transit systems (buses, airport shuttle, railway, subways, and cabs). This will facilitate inter-modal transfers into the existing plan of the city, which will in-turn reduce congestion and the unpleasant feel which commuters might experience.
